Donations

New Zealand birds are at risk of extinction due to predation by pests. Supporting conservation efforts can help save these species.

Pests

Ferrets eat eggs and young chicks

Rats eat young chicks

Cats hunt birds and their young

Possums destroy habitats

By donating, you can help protect these endangered birds from invasive species and ensure their survival for future generations.
